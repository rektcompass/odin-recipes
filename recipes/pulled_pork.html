Pulled Pork

Description

Fall off-the-bone pork that's easy and delicious!.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Barbecue Sauce

Steps

  1. Set slow-cooker to "low"
  2. Place pork in cooker, cover in BBQ sauce
  3. After ~4 hours, flip the pork and cover again in BBQ sauce
  4. After 7 hours total cook time, pull pork apart and mix within the slow-cooker
  5. Cook for 1 more hour, serve
