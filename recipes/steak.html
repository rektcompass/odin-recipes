Awesome Steak

Description

Juicy-ass steak for your face to enjoy any time.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Remove steak from refrigerator, rest for 20 minutes
  2. Heat butter in pan on stove
  • Generously salt and pepper each side of the steak
  • Add steak to the hot pan, flip after 10 minutes
  • Flip again after 10 minutes, remove from heat
  • Let steak rest for 5-10 minutes, serve
