Godly Chicken Tenders

Description

This is a recipe for wonderful, crispy, fried chicken tenders. This is a great recipe as it can easily be altered to feed any number of people. You can even add spices and sauces to fit particular cravings! In order to make this meal gluten-free, simply substitute gluten-free breadcrumbs!

Ingredients

  • .5LB Chicken cut into strips
  • 1 TBSP Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Time
  • Extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Add eggs and milk into a dish, whisk until combined
  3. Mix breadcrumbs and spices into a separate dish, hand mix
  4. Add olive oil to fry pan, heat on stove
  5. Lay 2 to 3 chicken strips in the egg wash
  6. Once the oil starts to cook, move the chicken strips to the breadcrumb mix and toss
  7. Make sure the chicken is coated, then add to the fry pan
  8. Repeat this process until the fry pan is filled with chicken, flipping strips in the pan every 2-3 minutes
  9. As strips reach 6 minutes, remove from the pan and place on a baking sheet
  10. After all strips are on the sheet, place in the oven for 30-40 minutes (until golden brown)
  11. Remove the chicken from the oven, let rest for 5 minutes, serve
